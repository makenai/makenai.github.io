His name is pronounced like [Pa-vewl Shim-chi-kov-ski](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WVZfC6PmXcI). If you prefer emoji, that's :paw_prints: :bell: :fried_shrimp: :hatching_chick: :coffee: :ski:.
